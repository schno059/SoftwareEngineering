Create and Explore

UMDAlive is an open-source project that drives to connect students by allowing them to create and join clubs. You may browse though our catalog of UMD clubs and find students that share your interests. Don't see a club you want to join? Have a new idea? Create your own club!

Stay Connected

UMDAlive allows you to stay connected with your friends by having club based pages that provide a place to schedule events, communicate ideas, and just plain have fun. You can hop over to your My Clubs page to see the clubs you are engaged in, where you can check out the latest activity on your favorite club pages.

Long-Term Goals

The long-term goal of UMDAlive, meaning the name is a bit of a misnomer, is to connect students from any universities, based on relative location. We hope to extend this platform to other Minnesotan universities, then to a national basis, and perhaps further if we can gather support and interest Thus, it is not our intention to center UMDAlive around the University of Minnesota, but rather use UMD as a base-line with which to grow from.
